Solomon is the longest member on staff. He has been head of HR for just over six years, which happens to be the majority of his life. His daily responsibilities revolve around an extremely strict sleep schedule. On average, 22 hours of sleep are necessary. Short breaks are allowed for bathroom breaks and chowing down on cat nibble. The rest of his time is spent meditating on ways he can find the time to sleep more. He leads by example and hopes others will learn to take life easy.
Stephen is the lone developer in the operation. After many years as a propriety stock trader he decided to switch gears and enter the field of web development. Currently he is in a 16 week program at Digital Crafts where he is learning full-stack web development. Prior to school he tried to learn as much as possible so that he can be an efficient and productive developer. So far most of his acquired skills revolve around Javascript, jQuery, HTML, and CSS. In the near future he will be jumping into the world of back-end development.
Bentley has been on staff for over five years. Her prior career involved guarding an apartment complex and roaming the streets alone. After being plucked away from the wild Bentley quickly adapted to her new life and role as head of security. She spends the vast majority of her day sleeping but any sudden noises are greeted with barking and extreme excitement. Should an intruder arrive Bentley greets them with extreme tail wagging and excitement.
